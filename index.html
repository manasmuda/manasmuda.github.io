MAZE WAR

Maze War is an online 5 vs 5 action fps game. The arena of the game is a maze which is randomly generated for each game. There are 2 teams in the game (Blue and Red) consisting of 5 players. The maze is randomly distributed with 15 items (let us call them ‘coins’). There is container placed at a team base. Every team must collect store these coins in these containers. The team with highest coins in container at the end of game wins the game..

This is still under development
